Why Campus Takes

Built for teams tired of professional respondents

Generic marketplaces are full of survey veterans who have learned exactly what to say. Our panel is the opposite: real students giving real first reactions.

Verified, not self-reported

Every panelist confirms current university enrollment through their .edu address before joining a single study. No box-ticking, no impostors.

Hand-picked, not crowdsourced

A person reviews every application and matches every participant to your screener. No anonymous pool, no gamed filters, no bots.

Small on purpose

Big marketplaces sell access to a crowd. We keep our panel small enough to actually know, and we vouch for everyone we send you.

Genuinely fresh perspectives

We cap participation frequency, so you hear first impressions rather than rehearsed answers.

Quality, guaranteed by process

How we keep the panel honest

We are a new panel, and a deliberately small one, so we will not show you borrowed logos. Here is what we will show you: the process that makes the numbers hold.

Enrollment checks at the door

Every applicant verifies a current .edu address before entering the pool, and we re-check each academic year.

Pay per completed session

If a participant no-shows and we cannot backfill the slot, you are not charged for it. Our incentives match yours.

Participants paid within minutes

Panelists receive payment right after finishing. Fast payment keeps the panel engaged, responsive, and reliable.

A human confirms every session

Double confirmations, reminders, and a screened standby on deck. This is how the show rate stays above 95%.
