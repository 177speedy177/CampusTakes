1. Who we are

Campus Takes is an independent research panel that connects verified university students with research teams for interviews, usability tests, and surveys. We are the data controller for the personal information described in this policy, which means we decide how and why it is processed. Our registered contact details are in the Contact us section.

Campus Takes is not affiliated with, sponsored by, or endorsed by any university or educational institution.

2. Information we collect

2.1 Information from students (participants)

When you join the waitlist, verify enrollment, or take part in a study, we may collect:

  • Identity and contact details: first and last name, university email address (.edu), and phone number (if you provide it).
  • Enrollment and eligibility information: your university, academic year, field of study, and confirmation that you are 18 or older and currently enrolled.
  • Verification data: information used to confirm your .edu email address and enrollment status.
  • Profile and screening data: responses to screening questions that help us match you to relevant studies.
  • Participation data: which studies you are invited to, accept, decline, complete, or miss.
  • Payment information: the details needed to pay you, processed through our payment provider (see Payments & tax information). For tax reporting, this may include your legal name and tax identification information.
  • Study contributions: your responses, recordings, and other content you provide during a study (see Research sessions & recordings).
  • How you heard about us: the referral source you select when signing up.

2.2 Information from clients

When you request participants or communicate with us as a client, we may collect:

  • Business contact details: your name, work email address, and company.
  • Study requirements: the participant criteria, format, timeline, and other details you share about your research.
  • Billing information: the information needed to invoice and collect payment for completed sessions.
  • Correspondence: records of your communications with us, including any call notes.

2.3 Information we collect automatically

When you visit our website, we and our service providers may automatically collect limited technical information such as your device type, browser, general location (derived from IP address), pages viewed, and referring links. See Cookies & analytics for details.

3. How we use information

We use personal information to:

  • Operate the panel: verify eligibility, match students to studies, and schedule and run sessions.
  • Fulfill client requests: recruit, screen, and confirm participants for research studies.
  • Process payments to students and billing to clients, and meet related tax obligations.
  • Communicate with you about studies, invitations, scheduling, your account, and support requests.
  • Maintain quality and reliability, including confirming attendance and managing standbys.
  • Improve our services and understand how our website is used.
  • Protect against fraud, misuse, and security risks, and comply with legal obligations.

  • Objection or restriction — object to or ask us to limit certain processing.
  • Portability — receive certain information in a portable format.
